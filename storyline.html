STORYLINE

In a quiet empty room, there was a young boy left with his empty “WHAT IS MY DREAM?” on the table. He fell asleep and the book turned its pages by itself.



When the boy was a child, he watched as the bird soared into the sky. The next moment, he was dragged out of the house by his parents, and all he saw was a row of hundreds of doors.



After visiting different doors, the boy was physically and mentally exhausted. He escaped and entered a random door. But that’s the moment he finds his dream.



Just as the boy found his first “dream” in life, he was immediately overwhelmed by heavy pressure. Thoughts and pressure mixed, troubling the boy like chaos. Was this just a dream?
